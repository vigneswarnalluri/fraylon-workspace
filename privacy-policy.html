Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Fraylon Technologies LLP ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Fraylon Workspace — our enterprise collaboration and workspace management platform.

Fraylon Workspace is designed exclusively for organisations and their authorised employees. The platform enables organisations to manage and streamline:

  • Task creation, assignment, and tracking
  • Team communication and collaboration
  • Organisation-wide announcements
  • Calendar and scheduling management
  • Push notifications and alerts
  • Employee profiles and role management
  • Departmental and workspace administration

By accessing or using Fraylon Workspace, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide, maintain, and secure the Fraylon Workspace platform. This information is provided directly by you or your organisation's administrator.

Account & Profile Information

  • Full name (display name)
  • Email address (used for authentication)
  • Employee ID and Firebase User ID
  • Organisation name
  • Department and team
  • Role and permission level
  • Profile photograph (optional, user-selected)

Workspace Activity Data

  • Tasks created, assigned, completed, or overdue
  • Announcements created or interacted with
  • Calendar events and scheduling data
  • Notification preferences and alert settings
  • App interaction data (e.g. screens visited, features used)

Device & Technical Information

  • Device identifiers used by Firebase services
  • Firebase Authentication tokens
  • App performance and crash diagnostics (anonymous)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise or approximate device location
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Device contacts or address book
  • SMS, call logs, or phone data
  • Photos outside of user-selected profile images
  • Microphone or audio recordings
  • Camera data except during voluntary profile photo selection
  • Browsing history outside the app

How We Use Information

Information collected through Fraylon Workspace is used solely for the following purposes and is never sold or rented to third parties:

  • Authentication: Verifying user identity and securing access to workspace accounts.
  • Workspace management: Enabling administrators to organise teams, departments, and roles.
  • Employee collaboration: Facilitating task assignments, team visibility, and workspace coordination.
  • Task management: Creating, tracking, and reporting on task progress and deadlines.
  • Calendar scheduling: Displaying and managing work-related events and deadlines.
  • Notifications: Sending relevant workspace alerts and announcements to authorised users.
  • Security: Protecting accounts, detecting misuse, and enforcing access controls.
  • Service improvement: Diagnosing technical issues and improving app reliability and performance.

Data Security

The security of your data is our highest priority. Fraylon Workspace implements the following measures to protect user information:

  • HTTPS encryption: All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS protocols.
  • Secure authentication: Firebase Authentication uses industry-standard token-based authentication to verify and protect user sessions.
  • Firebase Security Rules: Firestore and Storage access is strictly controlled by server-side security rules, preventing unauthorised reads or writes.
  • Role-based access control (RBAC): Users can only access data and features corresponding to their assigned role (e.g. Employee, Manager, CTO, Admin).
  • Limited administrator access: Only authorised organisation administrators may view or modify user records.
  • Regular security improvements: We continuously review and update security measures to address emerging threats.

While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely immune to risks. We encourage users to maintain strong, unique passwords and report any suspected security concerns to fraylontechnologies@gmail.com.

Data Retention

Fraylon Workspace retains user data for as long as the user's organisation maintains an active workspace account on the platform.

  • User profiles and account data are retained while the associated organisation account remains active.
  • When an employee account is deactivated or removed by an administrator, associated personal data may be deleted or anonymised in accordance with the organisation's data management policies.
  • Workspace activity data (tasks, announcements, calendar events) may be retained for organisational record-keeping purposes at the discretion of the organisation administrator.
  • Users wishing to request deletion of their personal data may do so by contacting their organisation administrator or submitting a request through our Account Deletion Request Form.

Your Rights

As a user of Fraylon Workspace, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that inaccurate or incomplete information be corrected. Many profile fields can be updated directly within the app.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data where applicable, subject to organisational and legal retention requirements.
  • Account deactivation: Account deactivation and removal is managed through your organisation's administrators.
  • Data portability: You may request a structured copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact your organisation administrator or reach us directly at fraylontechnologies@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Fraylon Workspace is an enterprise platform intended solely for organisations and individuals aged 18 years or older. The app is not directed toward children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18.

If we become aware that personal data has been collected from a person under 18 without verification of parental consent, we will take prompt steps to delete that information. If you believe we may have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at fraylontechnologies@gmail.com.
